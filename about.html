About Papaji Photography

Welcome to Papaji Photography

At Papaji Photography, we don’t just take pictures — we preserve emotions, moments, and memories that speak louder than words. With an eye for detail and a heart for storytelling, we specialize in capturing life’s most genuine expressions through our lens.

Whether it’s the quiet joy of a wedding, the vibrant celebration of a cultural event, or the elegance of a personal portrait, our mission is to turn fleeting moments into timeless stories. Let us help you relive your most beautiful memories, frame by frame.

Meet Viju Khatri

Founder & Lead Photographer, Papaji Photography

Viju Khatri is the creative force behind Papaji Photography — a name trusted for transforming moments into masterpieces. With an eye for detail and a heart for storytelling, he captures the raw, the real, and the radiant.

From destination weddings to heartfelt portraits, Viju’s lens narrates stories with authenticity and artistry. Each click reflects his passion for freezing time in the most beautiful way possible.
